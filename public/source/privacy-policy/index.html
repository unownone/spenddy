Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Spenddy is an entirely client-side application. All data processing happens in your browser — nothing is sent to our (or anyone else's) servers.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, track, or store any of your personal data. The Swiggy order JSON you load (either through manual upload or via the companion Chrome extension) never leaves your device.

2. No Account, No Analytics

Spenddy does not require an account, login, cookies, or analytics trackers. We do not embed third-party scripts that could compromise your privacy.

3. Open Source

The entire codebase is open-source on GitHub and licensed under the MIT License. Anyone can audit the code to verify our claims.

4. Local Storage

For convenience, Spenddy uses the browser's localStorage to cache your processed data so you don't have to re-upload or re-process it on every visit. You can clear this cached data at any time from within the app ("Clear Data" button) or via your browser settings.

5. Contact

Have questions about this policy? Please open an issue on GitHub and we'll be happy to clarify.
